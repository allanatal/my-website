Post 5 — #4 REDMOD AI for Pancreatic Cancer Detection
727 chars
4️⃣ #4 — REDMOD: AI Detects Visually Occult Pancreatic Cancer ~475 Days Pre-Diagnosis Gut | Mukherjee S et al. (Mayo / MD Anderson / U Washington) | April 28, 2026 • Trained on 969 patients; tested independently on 493 (1:6 prevalence — realistic screening) • AUC 0.82, sensitivity 73.0%, median lead time 475 days • Nearly 2× the sensitivity of expert radiologists (38.9%; p<0.001) • At >24-mo lead time: 68.0% vs 23.0% (radiologists) • Strong longitudinal stability (90–92%) and external validation in 2 cohorts (n=539, n=80) Pre-clinical interception in high-risk PDAC may finally be within reach. Prospective trial next. #PancreaticCancer #EarlyDetection #AI #Radiomics #GIOnc https://doi.org/10.1136/gutjnl-2025-337266
